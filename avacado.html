Quick Description:

Avocado has a high fatty acid content but contains
extremely low levels of cholesterol. Many people
tend to stop eating avocado due to its high-calorie
content, thinking that it may add to their weight.
However, a number of calories is very small when
compared to butter, and other high-calorie dietary
items. Also, much of the fat content comes from unique
sources, like phytosterols, which are beneficial for a
number of reasons.
